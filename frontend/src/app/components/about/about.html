I am Emma.

Italian, brown hair, green eyes.

Currently based in Lisbon.

I started photography in 2023, though my eyes had been training to be a photographer long before.

At 15, my father placed a camera in my hands, a gift I wasn't ready to hold yet. But moving to Portugal awakened me, and lit a fire that still burns bright.

My focus? Nature, humans, surf. Nothing stillness can’t touch, and nothing movement can’t blur. Still figuring it out, but enjoying every step.

Words may slip from me, but images hold me steady. Photography found me, and through its lens I speak.

I hope we speak the same language, and that you will understand my message. And if you find your own way to say it, even better.
